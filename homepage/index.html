About Us

La Consolacion College Tanauan (LCCT) is a Catholic educational institution founded to provide quality education that nurtures the mind, heart, and soul. With a mission to foster academic excellence and values formation, LCCT stands as a premier choice for students aspiring to reach their full potential.

We believe in holistic development through a combination of rigorous academic programs and dynamic extracurricular activities. Discover more about our academic offerings below.
