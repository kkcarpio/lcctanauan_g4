College

Description of College programs, offering various degrees and professional courses.

Courses Offered:

  • Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA)

    • BSA is a four-year degree program that combines accounting, finance, and management. Students are trained to manage a company’s financial information and tax documents. This program can serve as a first step toward licensure as a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).

  • Bachelor of Science in Management Accounting (BSMA)

    • BSMA is a four-year degree program that combines accounting, finance, and management. BSMA is a profession that involves partnering in management decision-making, devising planning and performance management systems, and providing expertise in financial reporting and control to assist management in the formulation and implementation of an organization’s strategy.

  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA)

    • A four-year degree program that is designed to develop students' comprehensive knowledge of marketing concepts to choose target markets and comprehensive skills in Human Resource Management.

  • Bachelor of Science in Psychology (BS PSYCH)

    • BS PSYCH is a four-year degree program that is designed to help students observe human behavior and its mental processes through the scientific method. The program offers broad scientific training with a concentration in Cognition and Cognitive Neuroscience; Developmental; Social; or Clinical and Behavioral Health.

  • Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED)

    • BSEd is an undergraduate teacher education program designed to equip learners with adequate and relevant competencies to teach in their chosen area of specialization in the secondary level.

  • Bachelor of Industrial Engineering (BSIE)

    • This program is designed to develop the technical skills and intellectual discipline needed by the graduates to become leaders in Industrial Engineering and related professions.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

    • The BSIT program is an in-depth exposure to computer hardware and software technologies. It prepares the students to be IT professionals who are able to perform installation, operation, development, maintenance, and administration of computer applications.

  • Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)

    • The BEEd program is an undergraduate degree program designed to prepare individuals intending to teach in the elementary level.

  • Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

    • BSHM is a four-year degree program designed to address the needs of the hospitality industry. This program leads to expertise in the management of hotel, food and beverage services, competencies in planning, organizing, implementing and evaluating MICE activities, risk management program to provide a safe and secure workplace.

  • Bachelor of Science in Tourism Management (BSTM)

    • BSTM is a four-year degree program designed to have a career in the field of Tourism and Event Management. This course leads to expertise in management of tour-operating agencies, airline operations and competencies in planning, organizing, implementing and evaluating MICE activities, risk management program to provide a safe and secure workplace.
